Visit Chihuahua

A colonial city in northern Mexico, Chihuahua is brimming with history and is the eastern terminus for the breath-taking Copper Canyon train (CHEPE).

Top three activities to do at Chihuahua

Travel through the Copper Canyon

The CHEPE train offers a unique and unforgettable journey through the stunning Copper Canyon region, allowing you to appreciate the breathtaking views of the deep canyons, rugged mountains, and scenic landscapes along the way.

Sandbording in the Dessert

Located less than 3 hours from Chihuahua's capital city, you will be able to go sandboarding at the Samalayuca dunes.

Explore Chihuahua city downtown

Whether you like to shop for crafts, be amazed by the beauty of a colonial downtown, or have dinner, Chihuahua city downtown provides an amazing experience.
